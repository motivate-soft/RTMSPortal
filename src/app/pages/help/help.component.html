Need help logging in?
1. Check your browser and version.
Real Time requires Internet Explorer 11+, Microsoft Edge, Google Chrome, or Safari.

2. Forgot your username or password?
Contact your Real Time Success Manager, call (888) 546-9786, Option 1, or send an email to help@realtimemed.com to get things resolved.

3. Is your account locked out?
After so many failed login attempts, your account will be locked out for a while. This is a security measure. Give it an hour and try again.

4. Location-specific log in protections
Your facility may have restrictions on which users can log in outside your facility. If so, and you are unsuccessfully trying to reach Real Time, check with your Real Time Success Manager.
